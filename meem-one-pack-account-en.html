Additional One Pack Account Features

Jam Jars

This enables you to save for a specific purpose during a period selected by yourself. Also, you can set-up a standing instruction to automatically set aside funds from the One-Pack account to a specific ‘Jam Jar’ at a selected frequency

Budgeting Tool

This enables you to budget and track your spending including a mapping tool to show where and what you have been spending

Automatic Sweep

This feature automatically moves funds between the One-Pack's current account and savings account, at the end of the day, according to your instructions

Sadad-Bill Payments

This feature enables you to have control over your bills; pay, view, and get notified when the bills are due to never miss paying bills on time!
