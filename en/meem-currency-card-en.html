Currency Card meem

Currency Card

The first of its kind in Saudi Arabia is the Currencies card which is a visa debit card offered free of charge card without annuals fees. The card can be linked to 4 different currencies designed for your convenience and saving when travelling abroad.

It's free!

Issued free of charge, and no annual fees

Charge free POS transactions

Absolutely no fees on point of sale transactions

Total Control

Depending on your needs, you may link and unlink foreign accounts to your currencies card anytime anywhere through meem's app or internet banking

Apply Now!

Additional Currency Card Benefits

Instant approval!

All meemeres can apply for the currencies card and they will get it immediately by collecting it in store or they can receive it by courier in 3 business days

Low fees on ATM withdrawals

Only 2.25 % is charged when withdrawing cash from an ATM

Variety of currencies

There are 8 options of currencies which can be linked to the card and they are; Bahraini Dinar, Emirati Dirham, Qatari Riyal, Omani Riyal, Kuwaiti Dinar in addition to Euro, British Pound, and American Dollar

Base currency feature

If the currency of your travel destination isn’t within the offered currencies, you can still use your currencies card by selecting a base currency that would act as your primary currency

Fast Exchange and Transfer Anytime, Anywhere

Currency Exchange Calculator

To avoid any surprises in the exchange rates when transferring funds to your foreign currency accounts, use the Currency Exchange Calculator to rates in real time.

Moving funds

To transfer funds into your foreign currency accounts select the icon on top right corner “Move Money” and select accounts you want to transfer funds from and to. Also, you will be able to see the current currency exchange rate before making the transfer.
