Thank you and welcome onboard!

WHAT TO DO NEXT?

  • Visit the nearest main meem store
  • Bring your registered National address (click here to register)
  • Get your Visa Debit Card on spot!
  • Don't forget to bring your National ID/Iqama and the mobile registered for authentication

    • Additional required documents:

  • Source of Wealth proof; In case your source of income is not a salary, please bring any of the following documents:
    • For sale of shares or Investment liquidation Income: a signed letter detailing the fund/Shares from regulated accountant or lawyer
    • For Sale of Property Income: a singed letter from estate agent
    • For Inheritance Income: Signed letter from lawyers
    • For Rental Income: Copy of leasing building
  • Proof of residence address: Bank statement of account or utility bill or telephone bill.

    • Note: P.O Box number is not an acceptable address.
