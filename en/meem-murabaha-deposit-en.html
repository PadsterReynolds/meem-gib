Applying for Murabaha Deposit

It's a very simple and straight forward process that won't take more than 5 minutes of your time. To start click on 'Your needs' from the left purple toolbar menu, then click on 'Murabaha Deposit'.

Selecting the currency

There are 9 different currencies to choose as your currency of deposit. To view the profit rates for each tenure ensure having a balance in the desired currency account.

Tenures & Profit Rates

We offer a range of tenures from 7 days to 720 days to suit your investment needs. The profit rates will depend on two variables which are the length of the investment and the invested amount.
