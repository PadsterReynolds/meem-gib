Assisted Service Terminal

Whether you are here to complete the account opening procedure or applying for a product, you will be escorted by an agent into a private and transparent room to get the proper service you need.

Self Service terminal

You will notice the absence of banking tellers at our stores, you won't need to bend and talk with people behind glass. Meem's self service devices enable you to withdraw and deposit cash, print statements, and perform all the banking functionalities you can think of.
